Julián Álvarez

Professional Football Player

Welcome to the official page of Julian Alvarez. He is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Atlético de Madrid and the Argentina national team. He is known as “La Araña”.

Career Highlights

  • He began his professional career at River Plate, winning the Copa Libertadores in 2018.
  • He was part of the Argentina team that won the World Cup final in 2022.
  • Later, he signed for Atlético de Madrid to continue his career in Europe.
  • Julián is recognized for his goals, speed and teamwork on the pitch.
